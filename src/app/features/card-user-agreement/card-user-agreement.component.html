Key Compliance Management Points for Card Issuers

1. Prohibited and Illegal Transaction Activities

The following transaction activities are strictly prohibited in this system:

  • Malicious or fraudulent submission of top-up information.
  • Abuse of free trials, including but not limited to trial services on Alibaba Cloud, gaming platforms, video websites, or similar services.
  • Using the card for prohibited payment scenarios as restricted by the issuing bank or this platform.
  • Using the card for PayPal, App Store, or other account verification purposes.
  • Using the card for services provided by government agencies, legal institutions, credit institutions, or similar organizations.
  • Using the card for any illegal activity, including but not limited to fraud or illegal transactions on platforms such as Airbnb, Lyft, Uber, or similar platforms.
Friendly Reminder: If any of the above violations are detected, the system may deactivate the card and close the account without refunding any remaining balance. Please use your card responsibly.

2. Card Chargeback and Failed Transaction Rate

  1. Please top up your card before making any transaction to ensure that the card has sufficient balance. You should also carefully verify the card information during payment to avoid transaction failures caused by incorrect information.
  2. If the card is no longer in use, you must first remove it from all related accounts and stop any advertising, subscription, or membership services linked to the card before canceling it. Please do not cancel the card directly in the system if there are failed transactions or if the card has not been removed from related accounts.
  3. Please monitor failed transactions in the system transaction details. If a transaction fails due to insufficient balance, you must top up the card immediately or remove the card from the relevant account.
  4. If the chargeback or failed transaction rate of a card segment reaches the platform’s risk control threshold, the system may suspend card opening, top-up, and balance inquiry functions for that card segment. If the rate reaches 30%, the system may automatically deactivate all opened cards within that card segment, and no refund will be provided.
